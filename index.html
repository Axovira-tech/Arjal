A Legacy of Divine Power

Historic Importance

The Arjal Shree Dhoomavathi Daivasthana holds immense historical significance in the Kumbla region. Situated centrally near Adhur, Madhur, Kapu, and Kasaragod, this sacred sanctuary has been renowned since ancient times for its unyielding miraculous power, blessing devotees and offering refuge for generations.

As we embark on the Punar Prathishta Mahotsava, we restore the divine aura and ancient glory of the Daivasthana through sacred rituals spanning four auspicious days. We welcome you to gather in devotion and witness this once-in-a-generation rejuvenation.

Concluding rituals, immense Prasada distribution, and Maha Annadana for thousands of devotees.
